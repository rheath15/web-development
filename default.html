In 1600s, armies used equations of motions to calculate velocities and angle for firing a missile to hit a target While a quarterback does not do explicit calculations using equationsof motion, a computerized football game will certianly need to do these calculations. We will use the metric notations that are favored for all scientific and engineering calculations.
